About - us

          We, 'Bappa Moraya Arts' started giving a online booking service of ganesh idols(Murti's) from this year via what's app. We are in this buisiness from past few years.

          if you are looking for the best selection of ganesh idols for ganesh festivals, you can find it on our website, we have a wide range of choices of ganesh idols in our website or you can visit our Store.

          Our idols are made from Red Soil, Shadu Mati, Paper pulp and Natural colours. they are completely eco-friendly idols. they are 100% dissolvable in water, and after visarjan soil can be used to plant a tree.
